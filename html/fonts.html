Sun tea

Today is a beautiful sunny day.

There are many things to do on a sunny day.

Such as prepare a refreshing sun tea.

I love to prepare my sun tea in the morning.
    Sun tea recipe:
  • Fill a glass jar with filtrated water.
  • Add 10 bags of lipton black tea.
  • Place the jar in the sun for 5 hours.
  • After 5 hours, in a pan add a little bit of the tea along with 10 pouches of sweet'n low and let it get to a boil.
  • Remove the bags of tea out of the jar and add the boiled sweet'n low into the jar and mix.
  • Serve with ice!
