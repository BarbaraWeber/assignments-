the best way I found to make a great sourdough bread is to have a really strong starter.
sourdough bread steps:
        1. I feed my starter and when it doubles in size and starts drop, it is time to use.
        2. Mix the water with the starter,then next add the flour and salt.
        3. Let the dough rest for 1 hour. after that, I start my stretch and fold combined with my coil folds every 30 minutes for 4 times.
        4. After the stretch and folds, I let it bulk ferment until it doubles in size.
        5. When it is already doubled in size, I shape the dough, place it in a banneton and then put it in the fridge for 12 hours. 
        6. Preheat and bake it in dutch oven.
